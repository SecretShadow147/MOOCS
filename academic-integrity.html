Academic Integrity Policy

At TB45.Courses, we are committed to building a fair, transparent, and high-quality learning environment. Academic integrity is the foundation of the value of the knowledge and certificates you receive. To protect our learning community, we strictly enforce anti-cheating policies.

Prohibited Actions

The following behaviors are considered serious violations of the integrity policy and will be handled accordingly:

  • Plagiarism: Copying assignments, ideas, or content from others without proper citation.
  • Cheating on exams: Using unauthorized materials, receiving help from others, or leaving the test screen without a valid reason.
  • Sharing accounts: Allowing others to use your account to study or take tests on your behalf.
  • Assisting in cheating: Helping others to commit fraudulent acts.

Measures We Apply

To maintain a fair learning environment, TB45.Courses uses advanced technologies to monitor and detect cheating:

  • Shuffling questions and answers: Exam questions are randomized so that each candidate has a unique set of questions and answer order.
  • Online Exam Proctoring: For important exams, we may require:
    • Webcam Monitoring (Simulated): The system will monitor you via webcam to ensure no one else is in the exam room.
    • Tab Switching Detection: The system will record and warn if you leave the exam window.
    • Anti-Copy/Paste: You cannot copy question content or paste content from outside.
  • Strict Time Limits: Each exam has a specific time limit and will be submitted automatically when the time is up.

Consequences of Violation

Any violation of the integrity policy will be dealt with strictly, including but not limited to the following forms:

  • Receiving a warning (Maximum of 3 times in one exam).
  • Cancellation of test or assignment results.
  • Cancellation of the entire course result.
  • Permanent account suspension from TB45.Courses.

We believe that adhering to these rules will help enhance the learning value for yourself and the entire community. Thank you for your cooperation.
